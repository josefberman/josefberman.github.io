About Me

I'm a Data Scientist at ICSGen.AI and PhD Researcher focused on swarm optimization and multi-agent reinforcement learning with applications in environmental modeling, chemical informatics, and autonomous systems.

My PhD research focuses on optimizing microswarm behavior in dynamic fluid environments such as blood vessels and pipe systems. I combine physics-based modeling with AI techniques to develop intelligent agent systems that can adapt to changing conditions.

My long-term goal is to become an expert in agentic AI systems for scientific exploration, creating autonomous systems that can help us understand complex phenomena and solve pressing scientific challenges.
