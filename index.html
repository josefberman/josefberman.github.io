About

Ph.D. student at the University of Haifa's Swarm and AI Lab

I'm pursuing a Ph.D. at the Swarm and AI Lab at the University of Haifa, exploring deep learning and reinforcement learning techniques for scientific applications. Proficient in authoring deep learning and reinforcement learning models.

My research focuses on optimizing microswarm behavior in dynamic fluid environments such as blood vessels and pipe systems. I combine physics-based modeling with AI techniques to develop intelligent agent systems that can adapt to changing conditions.

As a Data Scientist at ICSGen.AI, I work on applying AI methods to solve complex scientific challenges in environmental modeling, chemical informatics, and autonomous systems. My goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical AI research and practical scientific applications.

Technologies: Python, PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX, scikit-learn, pandas, NumPy, R, SQL, MLOps, Docker, Git, AWS
